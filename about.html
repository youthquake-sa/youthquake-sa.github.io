About Us

Youth Quake is a registered non-profit organization (Reg NO 043-958 NPO), an initiative of Burning Bush Ministries(BBM), established in 1998. It is spread to almost all the provinces in South Africa.
Youth Quake is born out of a need and compassion to address educational, social and economic challenges facing the Youth of South Africa. Youth Quake is about young people reaching out and helping, guiding and mentoring other young people in a right direction.
