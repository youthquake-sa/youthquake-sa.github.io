Youth Quake

Programs

Youth Quake is dedicated in conducting the following Programs for the young people.

School Outreach Program Defined objective

Youth Quake provide assistance mainly in disadvantaged, academically under-performing and under-resourced high schools. The school outreach program is designed to provide academic support, motivation, mentoring and life skills awareness.



Community outreach program

Awareness campaigns: Conduct campaigns on social ills in our communities like crime, domestic violence, woman & child abuse, xenophobia, drugs and alcohol abuse.
Sport involvement: promoting sport involvement by having organized and themed sport tournaments in our communities.
Skills development: assisting out of school youth on skills development in partnership with some of the SETA’s.
Stakeholder collaboration: partnering with relevant stake holders in the adopted communities such as SAPS, Correctional Services, NICRO, department of social development, department of sport recreation arts & culture
Quake Saturdays: doing acts of kindness in our communities such as clean-up campaigns, old-age home visits and renovations in a school or house of the Elderly.
Rehabilitation centre program: conduct life skills, motivational talks, health and wellness program at rehabilitation centres for young offenders.



Health and wellness Program

I am me, not for me: effects of drugs and alcohol abuse on the vital organs of the body.
Keep it healthy: nutrition, exercise and eating disorders.
Know your space, identify the hazards: Teenage pregnancy.
Who am I: Personality Disorders.
Dealing with it: Stress and anger management.
Stress factor: disease adaptation and coping mechanisms.
I know, I’m in control: Anger and anger management.
I have overcome: Dealing with Grief and Loss.
Yes I can! Know yourself and worth: Self-esteem and personal vision.
Tell it like it is: motivation and life talks from real life experiences.
Boot camp: character building activities and team building.



Lean on me- mentorship program

Academic excellence: through mentor-to-mentee monitoring, career guidance and support.
Moral reawakening
Personal Mastery: Personal vision, seeing reality objectively, being futuristic, promoting usage of teen energy and time on things that will positively affect their vision and future.
Inspiring Discipline



Campus Youth Quake

The Campus Youth Quake is the platform that is aimed at developing students to be responsible citizens an incubator of patriotism. It is the platform whereby the students are to be engaged on all the progressive student affairs, whilst in the main promote academic balance and sense of giving back to the community.



Youth Quake Tour

The YQ Tour is birthed from concern based on observation and study of students in the University of Higher Learning mostly from Rural and poor communities who get their careers shattered because of making wrong high school subject choices, not meeting required course prerequisites, late or not applying on time making them victims of choosing courses based on availability, financial constraints, not well informed on important issues such as different career choices, NSFAS and also becoming victims of lifestyle of Varsity life.



Holiday Mission Trips

South African Universities of higher learning host thousands of international students each year. There’s a tremendous opportunity to meet the nations at our doors, this was evident in the National Youth Quake Varsity Conferences held at NMMU in 2015 and 2016 respectively. Youth Quake has managed to be a cross cultural disciple making catalyst without stepping onto an airplane, this is evident to several international students reached in Universities of higher learning. As we build relationships with internationals, we can proclaim the gospel, make disciples, and inspire them to do the same. We can cast the vision of the Burning Bush Ministries for them to take the gospel message back to their own hometowns. The Holiday mission trip objective is based on identifying internationals who are members within the Burning Bush Ministries and our Campuses in the short term. Youth Quake has planned the Holiday Mission Trip as to great the platform for establishing the Church though using Youth Quake as the entry and evangelism strategy.
